A Letter

Well, I guess this is the final letter you’ll receive from me while you’re in Australia. I’ve sat here for a while, not knowing exactly what to write. Where do I begin? What can I say that I haven’t already said to you?

So, I thought I’d focus this letter on me for once and not you! and I can let you know how I feel about you, how you make me feel, and how you’ve impacted my life.

The first time I ever laid eyes on you was when you were sitting in that white chair in my living room back in June, wearing a beautiful red dress and with your gorgeous blonde hair. My first thought was, Damn, who’s this girl? She’s so fucking beautiful. It made me a little nervous. My introverted side came to the forefront, and I didn’t say anything to you because I didn’t know what to say without tripping over my words and blushing. Of course, it was you who spoke to me first and introduced yourself on the train, haha.

As I got to know you throughout the day, I remember slowly realising you weren’t just a pretty girl in a red dress. You were kind, funny, up for adventure, and someone whose company I genuinely enjoyed. I think that bottomless brunch in Bristol was the best £35 I’ve ever spent. 😊

Fast forward a few months, and I started to realise I was slowly falling in love with you. Any time I got a text from you, I’d catch myself smiling. The thought of meeting up with you made me genuinely happy inside. I realised that, before I met you, something had been missing from my life, and I was beginning to understand that you were that something.

Honor, I know you don’t see how amazing you are. You keep asking me: Why me? or What have I done to deserve this love? You often can’t see how anyone could love you. :( To me, this boggles my mind. The questions I ask myself are: How could anyone not love you? How could anyone not want to spend time with you? You are genuinely the most caring, loving, and kind person I’ve ever met. You take on the burden of looking after everyone else before yourself.

The truth is, Honor, you make me so fucking happy. You make me feel emotions I’ve never felt before in my entire life. You make me smile; you make me laugh. The thought of you not being in my life fills me with a horrible sadness. I genuinely couldn’t imagine my life without you in it.

I want to spend the rest of my life with you. I want to wake up almost every morning with you beside me, your head on my chest, my fingers running through your hair. I want to travel and experience the world with you. I want to marry you. I want to start a family with you. I want to live with you. I want to grow old with you. I want to hear your laugh and your voice every single day. The thought of all this makes me happier than you’ll ever know.

As I’m writing this letter, I’ve become more and more emotional to the point where there are literally tears in my eyes. But they’re tears of happiness and love for you. I want to tackle this world together with you.

Love you 3 trillion,

Alex
