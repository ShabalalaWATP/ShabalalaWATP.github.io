A Letter

Well, today is Sunday, the 3rd of November, 2024, and it has been exactly 33 days since I hugged and kissed you goodbye at the airport. I know deep down you had worries and doubts in your mind about your decision to do this, about your safety, about how you would cope on your own out there. You worried about how your friends would manage without you and, perhaps, even wondered if I would still love you and want you when you returned.

To be honest, I shared some of your worries. I have been concerned about your safety, and I had small doubts about how you would cope out there alone, especially considering your health. I've also worried that thoughts of your friends back in the UK might hold you back from truly enjoying this experience.

But let me tell you with absolute certainty, the one thing I have never doubted, not even for a moment, is how much I love you and that I want to spend my life with you, to grow old together. You being away for three or four months could never change that. I truly believe I have found "The One" in you.

And as for those worries and doubts, you've done so incredibly well. You had nothing to fear. You've travelled across the globe by yourself and absolutely smashed it. You've made new friends, maybe even ones for life and experienced things that 99% of people only dream about. It may not feel like it because of how busy you've been, but I think you've had a real break, a chance to get away from those 13-hour hospital shifts.

I am so unbelievably proud of you, and it fills me with happiness to see you experiencing so many amazing things, to see you happy and smiling.

Honestly, I do find myself feeling sad and emotional sometimes. It's something I've never really experienced before, and I don't like admitting when I feel vulnerable; it's just not something I'm used to. But I've realized that I'm emotional because I love you so deeply.

I know you're worried about moving back in with Ebony when you return, but I want you to know that you are ALWAYS welcome at my place. Even if I'm at work, I want you to feel at home when I'm not there. You are never a burden or an annoyance, even if you're up for work at 0630. I CAN'T WAIT until you're with me again.

Eventually, we'll find somewhere to call home together. A place where you'll have room to paint, where we can fuck as loudly as we like in every room we like, and where we can fill the fridge with all the vegan stuff you could desire! A place where we can be truly happy together.

Lots of love,
